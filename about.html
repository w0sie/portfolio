My Approach

Player-Centric Storytelling

I design narratives that respect player agency and investment. Every choice should feel meaningful, and the story should adapt to reflect the player's decisions and playstyle.

Cohesive Worldbuilding

Worlds should feel lived-in and consistent. I develop comprehensive lore and documentation that serves the story, ensures consistency across the team, and provides depth for players who want to dig deeper.

Collaborative Design

Great games come from great teams. I work closely with designers, programmers, artists, and audio professionals to ensure the narrative enhances and is enhanced by all aspects of the game.

Iterative Refinement

Narrative design is an iterative process. Through playtesting, feedback, and revision, I refine stories until they achieve the desired emotional impact and player experience.
